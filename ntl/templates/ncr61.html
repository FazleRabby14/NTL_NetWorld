NCR Selfserv TM 61

Interior Lobby Pocket Cash Recycler

Today’s ATM market brings new challenges for financial institutions, from increased consumer expectations, to ATM network security. However, one constant is consumer demand for cash which continues to increase year on year. With the introduction of new technologies, the ATM channel remains as relevant as ever in today’s omni-channel environment.
The SelfServ SE Cash has been designed to enhance the consumer experience, reduce costs and generate revenue. It does so by allowing you to: Connect with your customer, Perform better than ever before, all whilst increasing ATM Security.

Experience

SelfServ 61 gives consumers an intuitive way to interact with your business and allows Financial Institutions to let their customers bank in convenient high footfall locations.

Efficiency

Helping Financial Institutions lower their total cost of ownership and meet wider corporate social responsibility objectives whilst delivering service excellence through Software tools.

Agility

Driving availability through the high-capacity Scalable Recycler (SR) with a market-leading 300* note bunch for deposits and withdrawals.
