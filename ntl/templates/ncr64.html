Digitally-inspired design taking your brand to the next level whilst adhering to the global accessibility standards. Powered by the new enhanced Scalable Recycler, NCR SelfServ 64 is the perfect solution to future-proof your ATM network

Technical Specifications

Product Dimensions

  • Height: 56.37” (1,432mm)
  • Width Safe: 20.47” (520mm)
  • Depth Safe: 36.6” (930mm) + Collar: 9.4" (240mm)
  • Weight CEN I Safe: 1,400 lbs (635kg)

Consumer Interface

  • Display - 15” LCD multi-touch, optional privacy filter, sunlight-readable
  • Dynamic Colored MEEIs, Dynamic Mood Lighting (optional)
  • Intelligent Pocket Shutter
  • Keyboard - EPP (PCI compliant) with optional Pin Shield
  • Tamper Resistant Card Readers - Smart Dip, MRCW
  • Contactless - Integrated reader (via card, smartphone, or smartwatch)
  • Biometrics - Fingerprint reader (optional)
  • Audio - Optional high-quality public audio or sound jack with volume control
  • Barcode Reader (optional)

Media Handling 3.0 Technology

  • Cash Recycling: Scalable Recycler 3.0, scalable from 3 to 6 cassettes
  • Up to 8 denominations recycling, up to 300 notes bunch size
  • Cassette Capacities:
    • Recycling Cassette: up to 3000 notes, 332mm
    • Deposit Cassette: up to 3300 notes, 363mm
    • Split Cassette: 700 notes (77mm) + 1300 notes (143mm)
  • Exception bin for unfit, suspect & counterfeit notes (75mm + 55mm + 10mm)
  • Foreign object management and secure transport
  • Check Deposit: Scalable Check Processing Module 2.0 (optional)
    • Storage capacity: 5 bunch up to 400 items
    • Endorse and return, RGB, Infrared, and Ultraviolet recognition
  • Coin Dispense: SmartHopper Coin Module (optional)
    • Up to 11 coin denominations
    • Storage capacity (standard): up to 11.3kg (24.9 lbs)

Printers

  • Graphic Thermal Printer
  • Journal Printer (optional)

Security

  • USB Protection and Parts Validation
  • Portrait Camera, Cash Camera (optional)
  • Consumer Mirrors (optional)
  • NCR ATM Endpoint Security Solutions (optional)
  • Skimming Protection Solution (SPS) (optional)
  • Range of Alarms (optional)
  • UL437 cabinet locks
  • Safes: CEN I, CEN III GasEx, CEN IV GasEx

Environmental

  • Exterior TTW: -31°F to 122°F (-35°C to 50°C)
  • Humidity: 10% to 80%
  • Interior TTW: 50°F to 104°F (10°C to 40°C)
  • Humidity – 20% to 80%

Servicing

  • Rear access
  • Self Service Assistant (SSA)
  • Unique adjustable TTW sleeve

Operating Platform

  • Intel Core - i3, i5, or i7
  • 8GB, 16GB, 32GB, or 64GB RAM
  • 240GB or 480GB SSD Hard Drive (with 2nd SSD)

Software

  • Windows® 10 ready
  • NCR Software Suite

Additional Features

  • Uninterrupted Power Supply
  • Bluetooth
  • Dual Wireless
